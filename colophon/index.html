Technology and Design

This site is built with Eleventy (11ty). I’ve tried a lot of different web frameworks, but I keep coming back to it. It’s simple, flexible, has a lot of useful plugins, and just gets the job done.

The visual design of this site is largely inspired by the BMFW and Craigslist. I’m a big fan of brutalist aesthetics. I try to keep the design simple so the focus stays on the content. Extra features are usually there to improve the reading experience.

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages and doesn’t use cookies or analytics scripts.

AI Disclosure

I hate AI-generated content as much as you do. All articles on this site are written by me. I use AI for editing and improving my writing, e.g. grammar correction, simplifying explanations, prose/stylistic improvements, but the main ideas and content are my own.
